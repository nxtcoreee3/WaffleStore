Before you begin

Prerequisites

Make sure you have these ready before installing WaffleStore.

📱

iPhone or iPad

Running iOS 16.4 or later. WaffleStore uses no exploits and requires no jailbreak.

🔐

Apple ID with 2FA

Two-factor authentication must be enabled on your Apple ID before logging in.

📦

A Sideloader

AltStore, Sideloadly, TrollStore, or any other sideloading method that works for you.

Step-by-step

How to downgrade an app

Follow these steps in order. The whole process takes under five minutes.

01

Prepare the app

Uninstall the app you want to downgrade from your device. To keep your data, offload the app instead of deleting it — go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage and tap Offload App.

💡 Offloading keeps your data; deleting wipes it.
02

Install WaffleStore

Download the IPA and sideload it using your preferred method — AltStore, Sideloadly, TrollStore, etc.

03

Log in with your Apple ID

Open WaffleStore, enter your Apple ID credentials, then tap "Send 2FA Code."

04

Enter your 2FA code

You'll likely receive a 2FA popup on one of your Apple devices. Tap Allow, note the six-digit code, and type it into WaffleStore. If no popup appears, try entering six random digits instead — this often works if you've used WaffleStore or MuffinStoreJailed before. Tap Log In.

💡 No popup? Try any 6 digits — it may still work.
05

Paste the App Store link

Find the app you want on the App Store, copy its link, and paste it into the field in WaffleStore.

06

Pick a version and wait

Tap "Downgrade App," choose the version you'd like, and let WaffleStore do its thing. Grab a coffee — it may take a minute.

Troubleshooting

Something not working?

Common issues and how to fix them.

App crashes when trying to log in
You likely entered the wrong 2FA code. Force-close WaffleStore from the app switcher and try logging in again with the correct code.
App doesn't progress when clicking "Log In"
Your Apple ID or password is probably incorrect. Force-close WaffleStore from the app switcher and double-check your credentials before trying again.
I don't receive a 2FA code after clicking "Authenticate"
Type any six random numbers into the field and proceed anyway. If you've previously signed into MuffinStoreJailed or WaffleStore — even on a different device — this likely still works because your session is cached.
The app crashes when I try to downgrade
You must have previously downloaded (purchased) the app on your Apple ID. If you haven't, download it from the App Store on any device first, then remove it. WaffleStore can only downgrade apps your account has access to.
"Safari can't open the page" error appears
Go to Settings → Apps → Safari, then under Privacy & Security, disable "Not Secure Connection Warning." Also check that you don't have any active VPN or custom DNS settings that might be interfering with the connection.
